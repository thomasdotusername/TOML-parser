Rules:
- Comments:
	- Must start with # and end at the end of the line.
	- Can be placed at the beginning of a line, or after code.
	- Must not be wrapped in quotation marks.
- Key Value Pair:
	- Must be in format {key}={value}.
	- Whitespace is ignored.
	- Key and value must be on the same line, some values may overflow to next line.
	- Must be newline or eof after each pair.
- Bare Key:
	- May only contain [A-Za-z0-9_-].
- Quoted Key:
	- Follow rules of basic or literal strings.
	- May be empty [""|''] (unlike bare keys).
- Dotted Key:
	- A sequence of bare or quoted keys joined by a dot.
	- Whitespace is ignored.

Patterns:
/#.*[\w\s]+$/ -- comment
/^#.*[\w\s]+$/ -- full-line comment
/^.+#.*[\w\s]+$/ -- end-of-line comment
/[\w\d]+\s*=$/ -- bare key
/^\[.+\]$/ -- table header
